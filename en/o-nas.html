About us

The story, people and values behind the "Masz Ci Kwiatek!" flower shop.

Our story

A passion for beauty, step by step

The "Masz Ci Kwiatek!" flower shop at ul. Krakowska 150 in Michałowice was born from a love of flowers and a desire to create moments people remember. Every bouquet that leaves our shop is the result of a careful choice of flowers, colour and form - crafted to perfectly convey the emotions you want to share.

Right next to the flower shop we also run Sklep Trzy Światy, where you'll find carefully selected add-ons and gifts - a perfect complement to any bouquet. We serve our customers in both Polish and English.

Thanks to our own flower vending machine, open 24 hours a day, fresh flowers are available in Michałowice at any time - even when the shop is closed.

Our values

What matters to us

Freshness and quality

We order flowers regularly from trusted suppliers, so every arrangement is fresh and long-lasting.

A personal approach

We tailor every bouquet to the occasion, taste and budget of the customer - with attention to the smallest detail.

Closeness and trust

We're a local, intimate flower shop - we value relationships with regular customers and their recommendations.
