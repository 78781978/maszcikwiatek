2. What data we collect

In connection with using the website, we may process the following data:

  • data provided in the contact form: full name, phone number, e-mail address, message content;
  • technical data collected automatically: IP address, browser type, device data, cookies;
  • data provided by phone or e-mail for the purpose of fulfilling an order.

3. Purposes and legal bases for processing

  • Handling inquiries and fulfilling orders - based on consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and taking steps prior to entering into a contract and its performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR);
  • Contact by phone and e-mail - based on the controller's legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), namely responding to inquiries received;
  • Website traffic analysis and cookies - based on the user's consent expressed through browser settings or the cookie banner;
  • Potential claims - based on the controller's legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4. Data retention period

We store personal data for the period necessary to achieve the purpose for which it was collected, and thereafter for the period required by law (e.g. tax law) or until any potential claims become time-barred.

5. Data recipients

Data may be shared with entities that support us in running our business, in particular hosting, e-mail and contact-form service providers, solely to the extent necessary to provide those services and on the basis of data processing agreements in place.

6. Rights of the data subject

Every person whose data we process has the right to:

  • access their data and receive a copy of it;
  • rectify (correct) their data;
  • erase or restrict the processing of their data;
  • object to processing;
  • data portability;
  • withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before its withdrawal;
  • lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (Poland) or their local supervisory authority.

To exercise the above rights, please contact us at: kontakt@maszcikwiatek.pl.

7. Cookie policy

The website uses cookies - small text files saved on the user's device - in order to:

  • analyze website visit statistics (analytics cookies - only after consent is given).

Users may change their cookie settings in their web browser at any time, including blocking or deleting cookies already stored. Restricting the use of cookies may affect some functionality available on the website.

8. Data security

The controller applies appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of processed personal data, including protection against unauthorized access, loss or destruction of data.

9. Changes to the privacy policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We will inform you of any significant changes by publishing a new version of this document on this page, along with the date of the last update.
