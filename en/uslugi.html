Our offer

Flower shop services

Flowers, emotions, beautiful moments - from an everyday bouquet to a complete decoration of your event.

Bouquets for every occasion

Birthdays, name days, anniversaries, "just because" - we compose classic and modern bouquets in a full range of colours, with the option of adding a card with your wishes.

Occasional arrangements

Table decorations, arrangements in baskets and vases for a First Communion, christening, birthday or anniversary - styled to match the character of the occasion.

Weddings and ceremonies

Wedding bouquets, hall decorations, corsages and boutonnieres, church and outdoor arrangements - complete floral styling for your big day.

Interior and event decorations

Floral decorations for offices, restaurants, corporate events and celebrations - bespoke arrangements tailored to the space.

Gifts with soul

At the neighbouring Sklep Trzy Światy you'll find carefully chosen add-ons - plush toys, chocolates and small gifts, the perfect complement to a bouquet.

Flower delivery

Pressed for time, or need delivery to a specific address? Call us - we'll work out the details and the most convenient way to pick up or deliver your order.

"Masz Ci Kwiatek" Flower Vending Machine

In the car park right outside our shop at ul. Krakowska 150 in Michałowice, our 8-compartment stationary flower vending machine is at your service. It's a convenient solution for anyone who needs a fresh bouquet outside store hours - at any hour of the day or night, seven days a week.

  • 8 compartments with ready-made bouquets and arrangements available on the spot
  • In-person purchase - pay directly at the machine
  • Perfect solution for an evening, weekend or last-minute occasion
